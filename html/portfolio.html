    About Me

    Hello! My name is Alejandro, I am a dedicated student pursuing a Bachelor of Arts degree at the University of Toronto. I am enrolled in the Communication, Culture, Information, and Technology (CCIT) program, with complementary minors in Political Science and English.

    In my studies, I have developed a strong desire to enter the field of marketing after learning about the intricacies and diversity of the field. The CCIT program has introduced me to the numerous skills that are required to excel in the industry. I have developed proficiency in marketing practices including photography and design extending to skills like web development and data analysis. In addition to my studies, I dedicated a lot of time to mastering the tools used to excel in the field. I have a strong foundation with Adobe Creative Cloud Applications, Microsoft Office, and coding languages and frameworks like HTML & CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and React. With these skills, I am confident I can contribute to captivating marketing campaigns that encapsulate one's brand.

    Furthermore, I take great pride in the soft skills that complement my knowledge in the field. I am comfortable taking on a leadership role and collaborating with groups of people. My competitive nature and discipline make me push myself to learn from others to become better in whatever activity I do. I am excited for any opportunities to learn and experience the ever-evolving field of Marketing.
